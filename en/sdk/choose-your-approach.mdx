---
title: "Choose your approach"
description: "Choose between raw blockchain data access via Portal or processed data with SDKs"
mode: "wide"

SQD provides two ways to access blockchain data: **Portal** for raw data or **SDKs** for processed data. If you choose SDKs, you'll then select between Pipes SDK (streaming library) or Squid SDK (complete framework).
