Participating in SQD Network requires SQD tokens. This guide shows you all the ways to acquire them.

## Important Information

<Warning>
  Always verify you're using the correct SQD token contract addresses to avoid
  scams.
</Warning>

### Official Token Contracts

- **Arbitrum:** [0x1337420dED5ADb9980CFc35f8f2B054ea86f8aB1](https://arbiscan.io/address/0x1337420dED5ADb9980CFc35f8f2B054ea86f8aB1)
- **Base:** [0xd4554BEa546EFa83C1E6B389ecac40EA999B3E78](https://basescan.org/address/0xd4554bea546efa83c1e6b389ecac40ea999b3e78)
- **Ethereum:** [0x1337420dED5ADb9980CFc35f8f2B054ea86f8aB1](https://etherscan.io/token/0x1337420ded5adb9980cfc35f8f2b054ea86f8ab1)

<Info>
  To interact with SQD Network contracts, your tokens must be on the **Arbitrum
  One** network.
</Info>

### Wallet Requirements

Use the [SQD Network app](https://network.subsquid.io) to interact with contracts. Supported wallets include:

- [MetaMask](https://metamask.io)
- [Rainbow](https://rainbow.me)
- [Coinbase Wallet](https://www.coinbase.com/wallet)
- Any wallet accessible via [WalletConnect](https://walletconnect.network)

## Purchase Options

### Decentralized Exchanges (DEXes)

#### Buy on Arbitrum (Recommended)

[Buy SQD on PancakeSwap](https://pancakeswap.finance/?chain=arb&outputCurrency=0x1337420dED5ADb9980CFc35f8f2B054ea86f8aB1)

<Tip>
  The WETH/SQD pool on PancakeSwap Arbitrum is the best-funded SQD pool across
  all DEXes, typically offering the best rates.
</Tip>

#### Buy on Base

[Buy SQD on Aerodrome](https://aerodrome.finance/swap?from=0x833589fcd6edb6e08f4c7c32d4f71b54bda02913&to=0xd4554bea546efa83c1e6b389ecac40ea999b3e78&chain0=8453&chain1=8453)

After purchasing on Base, bridge your tokens to Arbitrum One:

**Fast Bridge:**

- [IceCreamSwap bridge](https://icecreamswap.com/bridge?chain=base) - Quick transfers

**Native Bridges (Lower Fees):**

- [Base → Ethereum](https://superbridge.app/base)
- [Ethereum → Arbitrum](https://bridge.arbitrum.io/?destinationChain=arbitrum-one&sourceChain=ethereum&token=0x1337420ded5adb9980cfc35f8f2b054ea86f8ab1)

<Note>
  Native bridges offer minimal fees but require up to 7 days for transfers.
</Note>
